Who is Guillermo?

Hi there! Nice to meet you all. I guess you're wondering who that tall, bearded classmate of yours is. Well, let me try to answer that question. Let's go!

My name is Guillermo Sáez Gómez. I am originally from Cadis, a small town located in the very south of Spain. I arrived in Belgium on Erasmus in 2015 and, like many others, never left. Came for the university, stayed for the beer people.

Computer science graduate?

Oh, no, no. Nothing like that, actually. In fact, my educational background has very little to do with software development. Okay, maybe a little.

I actually studied translation and conference interpreting, and music. In fact, came to Brussels to study at the Conservatoire Royal de Bruxelles. Then I finished my studies, and started working as a translator and conference interpreter.

During lockdown, I started toying around the idea of a career change. I thought of a few possibilities, including software development, looked up some information about each one of them and... Facebook did the rest. God bless those algorithms!

Last ten years

Okay, so basically:

  • 2011: Started studying translation and interpreting in Seville
  • 2012: Started studying music, also in Seville
  • 2013: Erasmus in Manchester, United Kingdom
  • 2015: Finished my BA in translation and interpreting
  • 2015: Erasmus in Brussels, Belgium
  • 2016: Started an MA in translation of software and multimedia products
  • 2017: Finished both my music studies and my MA
  • 2017: Started working as a freelance translator
  • 2018: Started an MA in conference interpreting
  • 2019: Finished my MA
  • 2020: Joined Switchfully!

Hobbies

My hobbies include languages, music, reading, travelling, going out, having a nice Belgian beer...
